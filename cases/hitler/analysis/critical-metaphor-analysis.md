## hitler-01-volk-racial-body

Mapping IDs: hitler-cmt-001, hitler-cmt-002, hitler-cmt-003, hitler-cmt-004, hitler-cmt-005, hitler-cmt-006, hitler-cmt-008, hitler-cmt-009, hitler-cmt-010, hitler-cmt-011, hitler-cmt-012, hitler-cmt-013, hitler-cmt-014, hitler-cmt-015, hitler-cmt-018, hitler-cmt-019, hitler-cmt-020, hitler-cmt-021, hitler-cmt-022, hitler-cmt-023, hitler-cmt-024, hitler-cmt-025, hitler-cmt-026, hitler-cmt-027, hitler-cmt-029, hitler-cmt-030, hitler-cmt-031, hitler-cmt-034, hitler-cmt-035, hitler-cmt-037, hitler-cmt-038, hitler-cmt-040, hitler-cmt-041, hitler-cmt-042, hitler-cmt-043, hitler-cmt-046, hitler-cmt-057, hitler-cmt-059, hitler-cmt-060, hitler-cmt-065

Persuasive function: Constructs the German people as a racial organism whose purity must be preserved against contamination and dissolution.

Relation to Koenigsbergian analysis: The Volk is the sacred object; its preservation against contamination is the law that legitimizes sacrifice and elimination.

Rival readings:

- Battle-victory is extended to denote the triumph of races, ideologies, or laws of nature; the competitive combat source domain is applied to politics and biology.
- Biological blood is extended to denote racial descent and inherited essence; the physical source domain underwrites racial-purity claims.
- Fate does not literally strike with a hammer, make decisions, or lead people; it is cast as an intentional agent.
- German `Schicksal` here may be a conventional expression for life-lot rather than a live personification.
- Individual mind is extended to collective cultural/civilizational agency; abstract spirit becomes a force that builds or degrades civilizations.
- Individual soul is extended to collective psychological disposition; the collective soul becomes something that political actors can capture or poison.
- Physical soil is extended to denote an organic territorial right; space becomes a biological necessity for the Volk.
- The botanical act of rooting out is extended to human elimination; the physical source domain is preserved in the compound.
- The exploited population is cast as a sacrificial victim consumed by a predatory agent.

Negative cases:

- Volk/racial-body language naturalizes what are political constructions; the biological framing should not be accepted as description.

## hitler-02-fuhrer-embodiment

Mapping IDs: hitler-cmt-028, hitler-cmt-036, hitler-cmt-048, hitler-cmt-049, hitler-cmt-054, hitler-cmt-058

Persuasive function: Makes Hitler the personal embodiment of the Volk's destiny, concentrating all agency and authority in his person.

Relation to Koenigsbergian analysis: The Führer is both the sacred object's guardian and its expression; sacrifice for him is sacrifice for the Volk.

Rival readings:

- Individual honor is extended to national-collective honor as a possessable, transferable property; the Reich's honor can be restored like a physical object.
- Individual soldiers or citizens are idealized through the hero category; heroism becomes the organizing concept for military self-sacrifice.

Negative cases:

- Führer-embodiment is an ideological construction; the corpus does not record resistance, dissent, or the coercive apparatus sustaining it.

## hitler-03-jew-parasite-disease

Mapping IDs: hitler-cmt-016, hitler-cmt-017, hitler-cmt-039, hitler-cmt-044, hitler-cmt-045, hitler-cmt-047, hitler-cmt-050, hitler-cmt-051, hitler-cmt-052, hitler-cmt-053, hitler-cmt-055, hitler-cmt-056, hitler-cmt-061, hitler-cmt-062, hitler-cmt-063, hitler-cmt-064

Persuasive function: Dehumanizes Jewish people as a pathological threat requiring removal, making genocide imaginable as hygiene.

Relation to Koenigsbergian analysis: Activates the enemy-as-bringer-of-death dimension at its most extreme: the enemy is not a rival people but a pathogen requiring elimination.

Rival readings:

- Physical destruction is extended to social/economic/racial elimination; the basic physical sense underwrites the metaphorical application to groups.

Negative cases:

- This cluster is not an analytic category but a record of genocidal ideology; its 'function' is the construction of the conditions for mass murder.

## hitler-06-sacrifice-martyrdom

Mapping IDs: hitler-cmt-007, hitler-cmt-032, hitler-cmt-033

Persuasive function: Converts political violence and death in the Nazi cause into sacred martyrdom, creating a cult of sacrificial obligation.

Relation to Koenigsbergian analysis: Directly activates the sacrificial-body dimension: movement dead become the sacred debt that authorizes further violence.

Rival readings:

- The individual gives up their ego/advantage to the community; this is not a religious ritual but is structured as one.

Negative cases:

- Martyrdom framing in this corpus is most developed in the Mein Kampf chapters; the public proclamations are more functional and less hagiographic.
